\subsection{Detailed Description}
Class for calculating the force between two particles caused by the Lennard-\/\-Jones-\/potential. 

This class implements the calculation of the force caused by the Lennard-\/\-Jones-\/potential. This force is calculated using the following equation\-: \[ F_{ij} = \frac{24 \cdot \epsilon}{\left( \left\lVert x_i - x_j \right\rVert_2 \right)^2} \cdot \left( \left( \frac{\sigma}{\left\lVert x_i - x_j \right\rVert_2}^6 - 2 \cdot \left( \frac{\sigma}{\left\lVert x_i - x_j \right\rVert_2} \right)^{12} \right) \cdot \left( x_j - x_i \right) \]
